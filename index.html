A Place to Stay..

   We have a 2-bedroom ground floor unit and a 3-bedroom small house that we let/rent to transients. Rate varies and depends on the number of people in the group as well as the season or time of the year such as Holidays, Panagbenga or Flower Festival, and Christmas Holidays. So when you send us an inquiry @ inadalou@yahoo.com, please let us know the actual number of people in your group/family including specific dates/month so we can give you the right price quotation and for us to check for availability of the place.
   Nearest landmark to us: Nevada Square, Panagbenga Park, Victory Liner Main Bus Station, SM Mall and Camp John Hay. Jeepneys are readily available at the basketball court which is just a few meters away from our house and they leave every after a 15-minutes interval whether they have passengers or not. Taxis do come by every now and then. It is only a short 5-minute ride by taxi from the main bus station of Victory Liner to our house and a 10-minute ride from our place to the City Public Market by jeepney at a cost of 8.50 pesos only.
